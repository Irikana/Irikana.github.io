May 10, 2026

Today, Knowledge Hall — a branch library (sub-library) of Shepherd's Library — is officially launched. Entries will be continuously added to the Knowledge Hall in the future. Since March 2, 2026, the prototype of the Knowledge Hall has been completed, first appearing on draft paper. Currently, knowledge in the Knowledge Hall is divided into three categories: Phenomenon, Traceable Knowledge, and Recallable Knowledge. You can enter the Knowledge Hall via the floating prompt at the bottom right after refreshing or re-entering the homepage.
